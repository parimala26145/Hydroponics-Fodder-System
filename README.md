# Smart Low-Cost Solar Operated Hydroponics Fodder System

## Project Overview

The **Smart Low-Cost Solar Operated Hydroponics Fodder System** is an innovative and sustainable agricultural solution designed to produce fresh, healthy, and nutrient-rich green fodder for livestock using minimal water, space, and energy.

Traditional fodder cultivation requires large land areas, high water usage, and long growth cycles. This project overcomes these challenges by implementing a **soil-less hydroponic technique** powered entirely by **renewable solar energy**, making it highly suitable for rural and resource-limited environments.

This system provides an eco-friendly alternative for farmers, dairy industries, and agricultural innovators by enabling fodder production throughout the year with reduced operational costs.

---

## Objectives

- To design and develop a **low-cost hydroponics fodder growth system**
- To utilize **solar power** for sustainable and uninterrupted operation
- To reduce water consumption compared to conventional farming methods
- To automate fodder growth monitoring and nutrient delivery
- To support livestock farming with fresh and nutritious feed production

## Working Principle

1. Seeds are placed in hydroponic trays without soil  
2. Water mixed with nutrients is circulated through the system  
3. Solar panels supply energy to run pumps and monitoring devices  
4. Sensors track environmental parameters for optimal growth  
5. Fresh green fodder is produced within a short cycle and harvested
